About Clarun IT

Founded in 2010, Clarun IT is a boutique consultancy specializing in mission-critical database solutions. Our team holds Oracle OCM, Microsoft MCSE, AWS & Azure certifications and has delivered projects for Fortune 500 clients across finance, healthcare and retail.

Our approach:

  • Assess: Comprehensive health-checks, performance baselining & cost analysis.
  • Design: Future-proof architectures, security hardening & compliance mapping.
  • Implement: Automated IaC deployments (Terraform, ARM templates), CI/CD pipelines & detailed cutover planning.
  • Support: 24×7 monitoring, proactive tuning sprints & quarterly roadmap reviews.
